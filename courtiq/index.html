Smarter courts,
fairer play.

CourtIQ helps clubs maximize court utilization with automated reminders and intelligent rules enforcement — so every member gets fair access.

Get Started

Currently available for ASRC members only.

How it works

Simple, automatic, reliable

1

Reservations synced

CourtIQ continuously monitors the club's reservation system for upcoming court bookings and usage patterns.

2

Reminders & checks

Members receive personalized SMS reminders before their reservations. Bookings are checked against club rules in real time.

3

Fair access ensured

Courts don't go to waste, rules are enforced consistently, and every member gets their fair share of court time.

Features

Built for club operations

⏰

Timely Reminders

SMS reminders arrive before each reservation with enough lead time to prepare or cancel — so courts don't go to waste.

📋

Rules Enforcement

Automatically monitors weekly allotments, daily limits, and gap rules. First violations trigger a notification; repeat violations are auto-corrected.

💬

SMS Cancellation

Members can cancel reservations directly by replying to their reminder — reducing friction and freeing up courts faster.

🔔

Availability Alerts

When a court opens up due to cancellation, interested members are notified in real time for their preferred time blocks.

🔗

Back-to-Back Merging

Consecutive court slots for the same member are automatically merged into a single reminder with the full block duration.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family-Based Tracking

Usage rules are tracked per family, not per individual — matching how clubs actually allocate court time.
