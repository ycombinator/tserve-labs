iOS App

Your USTA NorCal league,
always in your pocket.

Net Results is the companion app for USTA NorCal tennis league players. Rosters, ratings, schedules, scores — all in one place.

Download on the App Store

Features

Everything your league season needs

🎾

Your Profile

See all your current, upcoming, and past league teams the moment you open the app. TennisRecord and UTR ratings displayed front and center.

📋

Roster & Ratings

Browse your full team roster with TennisRecord and UTR ratings under every name. Sort by rating to plan your lineup before match day.

📅

Schedule & Scores

Upcoming matches with time, location, and match notes. Tap any result to see the full line-by-line scorecard with player ratings.

✅

Availability

Mark your availability for each match date directly in the app — no more replying to group texts.

📤

Lineup Sharing

Send your lineup to the opposing captain and receive theirs — all without leaving the app.

🏆

Enter & Verify Scores

Submit match scores and verify your opponent's scores after match day — right from your phone.

All your teams, one view

Log in with your USTA NorCal account and instantly see every team you're on — current, upcoming, and past seasons.

  • Win–loss record at a glance
  • Next match date and opponent shown on each card
  • TennisRecord, UTR-S, and UTR-D ratings on your profile
  • Tap any team to dive deeper

Know who you're playing with — and against

The full roster with TennisRecord and UTR ratings for every player. Tap any player to see their match history and individual record.

  • Sort by name, TennisRecord, or UTR
  • View opponent rosters before match day
  • See win–loss record and match results per player

Every score, every line

Tap any match result to see the full scorecard — who played each line, who they played against, the score, and everyone's ratings.

  • Line-by-line match results
  • TennisRecord and UTR shown for all four players on each line
  • Enter and verify scores after match day

Never miss a match day detail

Upcoming matches show the time, location, match notes from your captain, and a place to mark your availability — all in one tap.

  • Match notes and gate codes from your captain
  • Mark Yes, No, Maybe, or Call Last
  • Home or Away indicator

Your ratings, your way

Choose exactly which ratings appear throughout the app — TennisRecord, UTR Singles, UTR Doubles, and ratings in match results are all independently toggleable.

  • Toggle TennisRecord ratings on or off
  • Show or hide UTR Singles and Doubles ratings
  • Control whether ratings appear in match scorecards
  • UTR options auto-disable if you skip UTR sign-in
